# Early Trust & Moderation Operations

## Manual Moderation

### Moderation Philosophy
- **Manual-First Approach**
  - All content manually reviewed
  - No automated approval
  - Human judgment prioritized
  - Quality over speed
  - Trust preservation

- **Moderation Standards**
  - Zero tolerance for violations
  - Fair and consistent moderation
  - Transparent moderation process
  - Clear rejection reasons
  - Appeal mechanism

### Moderation Workflow
- **Content Intake**
  - Automated flagging (assistance only)
  - Manual review queue
  - Priority assignment
  - Reviewer assignment
  - Timeline setting

- **Manual Review**
  - Content examination
  - Quality assessment
  - Policy check
  - Trust assessment
  - Decision making

- **Decision Process**
  - Approval (meets all standards)
  - Rejection (violates standards)
  - Request changes (minor issues)
  - Hold (needs clarification)
  - Escalation (complex cases)

### Moderation SLAs
- **Standard Content**: 24 hours
- **Priority Content**: 12 hours
- **Urgent Content**: 4 hours
- **Complex Cases**: 48 hours

## Trust Preservation

### Trust Measures
- **Trust Scoring**
  - Initial trust score (based on verification)
  - Performance-based adjustments
  - Customer feedback integration
  - Trust score tracking
  - Trust-based visibility

- **Trust Actions**
  - Trust score > 80: Full visibility
  - Trust score > 60: Standard visibility
  - Trust score > 40: Limited visibility
  - Trust score < 40: Restricted visibility
  - Trust score < 20: Suspension
