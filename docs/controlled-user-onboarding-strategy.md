# Controlled User Onboarding Strategy

## Onboarding Philosophy

### Controlled Growth Principles
- **Quality Over Quantity**
  - Prioritize quality over volume
  - Manual verification for all
  - Strict quality gates
  - Performance-based expansion
  - Trust-based growth

- **Gradual Expansion**
  - Start with premium users
  - Expand to trusted users
  - Gradual volume increase
  - Quality-based scaling
  - Risk-based growth

- **Trust Preservation**
  - Verify all users
  - Monitor all users
  - Remove low-quality users
  - Maintain ecosystem trust
  - Protect ecosystem quality

## Vendor Onboarding Limits

### Week 1-2: Premium Vendors
- **Onboarding Limit**: 5 vendors per week
- **Total Limit**: 10 vendors
- **Onboarding Method**: Invite-only
- **Verification**: Manual verification required
- **Quality Gates**:
  - Business license verification
  - Portfolio review
  - Reference checks (minimum 3)
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (5 listings)

### Week 3-4: Trusted Vendors
- **Onboarding Limit**: 10 vendors per week
- **Total Limit**: 30 vendors
- **Onboarding Method**: Referral-based
- **Verification**: Manual verification required
- **Quality Gates**:
  - Business license verification
  - Portfolio review
  - Reference checks (minimum 2)
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (5 listings)

### Week 5-8: Controlled Expansion
- **Onboarding Limit**: 15 vendors per week
- **Total Limit**: 90 vendors
- **Onboarding Method**: Application-based
- **Verification**: Manual verification required
- **Quality Gates**:
  - Business license verification
  - Portfolio review
  - Reference checks (minimum 1)
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (5 listings)

### Onboarding Criteria
- **Minimum Requirements**:
  - Valid business license
  - Professional portfolio
  - Positive references
  - Quality products
  - Fair pricing

- **Rejection Reasons**:
  - Invalid business license
  - Poor portfolio quality
  - Negative references
  - Low quality products
  - Unfair pricing

## Quality Control

### Quality Gates
- **Pre-Onboarding**
  - Application review
  - Document verification
  - Reference checks
  - Quality assessment
  - Risk assessment

- **During Onboarding**
  - Trial period monitoring
  - Performance tracking
  - Quality monitoring
  - Trust scoring
  - Adjustment based on performance

- **Post-Onboarding**
  - Continuous monitoring
  - Performance tracking
  - Quality assessment
  - Trust scoring
  - Removal if quality drops

### Removal Criteria
- **Immediate Removal**
  - Fraudulent activity
  - Security violations
  - Trust violations
  - Severe quality failures
  - Legal violations

- **Probation**
  - Quality drops below threshold
  - Performance drops below threshold
  - Trust score drops below threshold
  - Customer complaints
  - Repeated violations

- **Gradual Removal**
  - Continued poor performance
  - Continued quality issues
  - Continued trust issues
  - Failure to improve
  - Ecosystem impact

## Monitoring & Adjustment

### Monitoring Metrics
- **Onboarding Metrics**
  - Onboarding rate
  - Approval rate
  - Rejection rate
  - Quality score
  - Trust score

- **Performance Metrics**
  - Performance score
  - Quality score
  - Customer satisfaction
  - Trust score
  - Overall impact

### Adjustment Strategy
- **Increase Limits**
  - High approval rate (>80%)
  - High quality score (>80)
  - High trust score (>80)
  - High customer satisfaction (>4.5/5)
  - Stable operations

- **Decrease Limits**
  - Low approval rate (<60%)
  - Low quality score (<60)
  - Low trust score (<60)
  - Low customer satisfaction (<3.5/5)
  - Operational issues

## Success Metrics

### Onboarding Metrics
- Onboarding success rate

### Ecosystem Metrics
- User satisfaction
- Vendor satisfaction
- Provider satisfaction

- Property owner satisfaction

## Goal

Avoid low-quality ecosystem flooding through controlled onboarding limits, comprehensive quality gates, performance-based expansion, and continuous monitoring for vendors, contractors, providers, sourcing partners, and property owners.
