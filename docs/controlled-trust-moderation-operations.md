## Trust Scoring

### Trust Philosophy
- **Trust Standards**
  - Trust is earned, not given
  - Trust is measurable
  - Trust is dynamic
  - Trust is transparent
  - Trust is actionable

- **Trust Factors**
  - Quality score (40% weight)
  - Performance score (30% weight)
  - Customer satisfaction (20% weight)
  - Compliance score (10% weight)

### Trust Scoring Process
- **Trust Calculation**
  - Real-time trust scoring
  - Historical trust tracking
  - Predictive trust modeling
  - Trust trend analysis
  - Trust risk assessment

- **Trust Actions**
  - Trust score > 80: Full visibility
  - Trust score > 60: Standard visibility
  - Trust score > 40: Limited visibility
  - Trust score < 40: Restricted visibility
  - Trust score < 20: Suspension

## Provider Discipline

### Discipline Philosophy
- **Discipline Standards**
  - Fair and consistent discipline
  - Progressive discipline approach
  - Clear violation definitions
  - Transparent discipline process
  - Appeal mechanism

- **Discipline Levels**
  - Level 1: Warning (first violation)
  - Level 2: Probation (second violation)
  - Level 3: Suspension (third violation)
  - Level 4: Termination (fourth violation)

### Discipline Process
- **Violation Detection**
  - Automated detection
  - Manual reporting
  - Customer complaints
  - Quality drops
  - Trust score drops

- **Discipline Actions**
  - Warning: Written warning, improvement plan, monitoring
  - Probation: Restricted access, increased monitoring, performance review
  - Suspension: Temporary suspension, investigation, final warning
  - Termination: Permanent termination, account closure, blacklisting
