## Moderation

### Moderation Strategy
- **Initial Operations (Week 1-4)**
  - Manual moderation for all content
  - 100% manual review
  - Quality assessment mandatory
  - Trust scoring mandatory
  - Performance tracking

- **Expanded Operations (Week 5-8)**
  - Manual moderation with AI assistance
  - 80% manual review, 20% AI-assisted
  - Quality assessment mandatory
  - Trust scoring mandatory
  - Performance tracking

- **Broad Operations (Month 3-6)**
  - Manual moderation with AI optimization
  - 60% manual review, 40% AI-assisted
  - Quality assessment mandatory
  - Trust scoring mandatory
  - Performance tracking

### Moderation Standards
- **Content Standards**
  - Accurate descriptions
  - High-quality images
  - Fair pricing
  - Proper categorization
  - Complete information

- **Quality Standards**
  - Quality score > 70 required
  - Trust score > 70 required
  - Customer satisfaction > 4.0/5
  - Zero tolerance for violations
  - Fair and consistent moderation

### Moderation Process
- **Content Intake**
  - Automated flagging (assistance only)
  - Manual review queue
  - Priority assignment
  - Reviewer assignment
  - Timeline setting

- **Manual Review**
  - Content examination
  - Quality assessment
  - Policy check
  - Trust assessment
  - Decision making

- **Decision Process**
  - Approval (meets all standards)
  - Rejection (violates standards)
  - Request changes (minor issues)
  - Hold (needs clarification)
  - Escalation (complex cases)

### Trust Scoring Standards
- **Scoring Criteria**
  - Verification quality (30%)
  - Performance quality (30%)
  - Customer feedback (20%)
  - Quality score (10%)
  - Trust history (10%)

- **Trust Actions**
  - Trust score > 80: Full visibility/priority

  - Trust score < 40: Suspension
