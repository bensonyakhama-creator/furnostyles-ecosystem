# Furnostyles Marketplace Ecosystem Architecture

**Version:** 1.0 | **Created:** May 23, 2026

---

## 1. Marketplace Category Structure

### 6 Primary Marketplaces:
1. **Furniture Marketplace** - Sofas, beds, tables, wardrobes, custom furniture
2. **Construction & Materials** - Building materials, tiles, paint, gypsum, plumbing, electrical, hardware
3. **Services Marketplace** - Interior design, repairs, carpentry, plumbing, electrical, painting, installations
4. **Real Estate & Property** - Sales, rentals, Airbnb setup, staging, property upgrades
5. **Secondhand Marketplace** - Used furniture, appliances, renovation leftovers, clearance
6. **Dropshipping & Sourcing** - Imported products, supplier-linked, external marketplace, affiliate

### Ownership Types:
- `furnostyles_owned` - Direct Furnostyles products/services
- `vendor_listed` - Third-party vendor products
- `provider_listed` - Third-party service providers
- `dropshipped` - Sourced products via supplier network
- `affiliate` - External marketplace referrals

## 10. Furnostyles-Owned vs Third-Party

**Visual Differentiation:**
- Furnostyles: Gold badge "Furnostyles Exclusive", premium placement, direct warranty
- Third-Party: Blue badge "Verified Vendor", vendor rating, vendor support
- Dropshipped: Purple badge "Sourced Product", supplier info, shipping estimates
- Affiliate: Green badge "Partner Product", external link, platform attribution

**Priority:** Homepage hero (Furnostyles only), category pages (Furnostyles top), search (Furnostyles first 3 results)

## 11. Commission System

**Product Commissions:** Furniture 10-15%, Materials 5-10%, Dropshipping 15-25%, Affiliate 5-15%

**Service Commissions:** Interior Design 15-20%, Repairs 10-15%, Installation 12-18%, Renovations 10-15%

**Real Estate Commissions:** Sales 2-5%, Rentals 50% first month rent, Airbnb Setup 10%, Staging 15%

**Payouts:** Monthly cycle, KES 5,000 minimum threshold, M-Pesa/bank transfer, 3-5 business days

---

## 12. Lead-Generation Strategy

**Service Flow:** User submits quote → Lead routed to providers → Providers respond with quotes → User selects provider → Commission on completion

**Real Estate Flow:** User submits inquiry → Lead routed to agents → Agent responds → User schedules viewing → Commission on transaction

**Lead Quality:** Verified contact, project details, budget, timeline, location

**Distribution:** Geographic matching, rating priority, availability matching, tier-based priority

---

## 13. Dropshipping Strategy

**Sourcing Flow:** User requests/browses → Furnostyles identifies suppliers → Supplier provides pricing → User orders → Supplier ships → Furnostyles handles support

**Revenue Models:** Markup (Furnostyles adds margin), Commission (supplier sets price), Hybrid (base + commission on premium)

**Risk Management:** Supplier verification, performance tracking, backup suppliers, quality assurance

---

## 14. Supplier Partnership Strategy

**Partnership Tiers:** Strategic (exclusive, priority), Preferred (featured, lower commission), Standard (basic terms), Trial (monitoring period)

**Benefits:** Access to customer base, marketing support, payment processing, analytics, brand association

**Agreement Terms:** Commission structure, pricing guidelines, quality standards, shipping requirements, return policies, marketing permissions

---

## 15. Vendor Onboarding Strategy

**Process:** Application → Verification → Approval → Onboarding

**Requirements:** Business registration, KRA PIN, ID, bank details, product catalog, pricing, insurance, licenses

**Support:** Training resources, marketing support, featured placement during launch

---

## 16. Product-Detail Page Strategy

## 17. Service-Request Strategy

**Flow:** User browses → Submits quote form → Lead distributed → Providers respond → User selects → Service delivered → Commission paid

## 18. Real-Estate Inquiry Strategy

**Form Fields:** Property ID, inquiry type, contact info, preferred method, message, optional budget/timeline

**Listing Features:** Title, type, price, location, details, description, amenities, photos, video, floor plan, agent info
