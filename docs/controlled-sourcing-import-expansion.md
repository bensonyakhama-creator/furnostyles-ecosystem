## Trust Preservation

### Trust Measures
- **Trust Scoring**
  - Initial trust score (based on verification)
  - Performance-based adjustments
  - Customer feedback integration
  - Trust score tracking
  - Trust-based visibility

- **Trust Actions**
  - Trust score > 80: Full priority
  - Trust score > 70: High priority
  - Trust score > 60: Medium priority
  - Trust score < 60: Low priority
  - Trust score < 40: No sourcing
