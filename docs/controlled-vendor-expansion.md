# Controlled Vendor Expansion

## Expansion Philosophy

### Controlled Growth Principles
- **Quality Over Quantity**
  - Quality-based expansion
  - Performance-based scaling
  - Trust-based growth
  - Premium marketplace
  - Ecosystem quality

- **Gradual Expansion**
  - Start with premium vendors
  - Expand to trusted vendors
  - Scale to quality vendors
  - Monitor continuously
  - Adjust based on performance

## Furniture Vendors

### Expansion Strategy
- **Initial Expansion (Week 1-4)**
  - 20 premium furniture vendors
  - Invite-only onboarding
  - Manual verification
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (10 listings)

- **Expanded Expansion (Week 5-8)**
  - 40 trusted furniture vendors
  - Referral-based onboarding
  - Manual verification
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (10 listings)

- **Broad Expansion (Month 3-6)**
  - 100 quality furniture vendors
  - Application-based onboarding
  - Manual verification
  - Quality assessment
  - Trial period (10 listings)

### Onboarding Criteria
- **Minimum Requirements**
  - Valid business license
  - Professional portfolio
  - High-quality products
  - Fair pricing
  - Positive references (minimum 3)
  - Quality certification (preferred)

- **Quality Standards**
  - Product quality > 80
  - Portfolio quality > 80
  - Reference quality > 80
  - Pricing fairness > 80
  - Overall quality > 80

### Quality Control
- **Pre-Onboarding**
  - Business verification
  - Portfolio review
  - Reference checks
  - Quality assessment
  - Risk assessment

- **During Onboarding**
  - Trial period monitoring
  - Performance tracking
  - Quality monitoring
  - Trust scoring
  - Adjustment based on performance

- **Post-Onboarding**
  - Continuous monitoring
  - Performance tracking
  - Quality assessment
  - Trust scoring
  - Removal if quality drops

## Trust Preservation

### Trust Measures
- **Trust Scoring**
  - Initial trust score (based on verification)
  - Performance-based adjustments
  - Customer feedback integration
  - Trust score tracking
  - Trust-based visibility

- **Trust Actions**
  - Trust score > 80: Full visibility
  - Trust score > 70: Standard visibility
  - Trust score > 60: Limited visibility
  - Trust score < 60: Restricted visibility
  - Trust score < 40: Suspension
