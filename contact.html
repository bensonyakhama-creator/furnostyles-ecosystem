FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and consultation process.

How do I request a consultation?

Contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email. We'll schedule a free consultation at your convenience. You can also fill out the inquiry form above and we'll contact you within 24 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas including Kiambu, Machakos, and Kajiado. We also deliver to major towns across Kenya. Contact us to confirm service availability in your location.

Do you provide quotes?

Yes, we provide free quotes after assessing your project requirements during consultation. Quotes include detailed breakdowns of materials, labor, and timeline estimates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash. Payment terms are discussed during project planning. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based payment schedules.

How long does a project take?

Project duration varies based on scope. Simple repairs may take 1-3 days, while full interior design projects can take 4-12 weeks. We provide timeline estimates during consultation.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, we offer warranties on our workmanship and materials. Warranty terms vary by service type and are specified in your project agreement. Custom furniture typically comes with a 2-year warranty.
