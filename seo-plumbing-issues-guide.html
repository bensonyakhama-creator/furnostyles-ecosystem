COMMON PLUMBING ISSUES

Frequent Plumbing Problems

Understanding common plumbing issues.

Leaking Taps

Worn washers and valves cause constant dripping and water waste.

Blocked Drains

Debris buildup causes slow drainage and complete blockages.

Running Toilet

Faulty flapper or fill valve causes continuous water flow.

Low Water Pressure

Pipe blockages or leaks reduce water flow significantly.

Pipe Leaks

Corrosion or damage causes hidden water leaks and damage.

Water Heater Issues

Sediment buildup or faulty elements affect hot water supply.

PLUMBING MAINTENANCE

Prevent Plumbing Problems

Expert tips for plumbing maintenance.

Regular Inspections

Check pipes, taps, and toilets regularly for early problem detection.

Avoid Clog-Causing Items

Don't flush wipes, grease, or food down drains to prevent blockages.

Monitor Water Bills

Sudden bill increases may indicate hidden leaks requiring attention.

Know Your Shut-Offs

Locate main water shut-off valve for emergency leak situations.
