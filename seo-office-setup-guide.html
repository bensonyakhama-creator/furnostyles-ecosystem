ERGONOMIC TIPS

Healthy Workspace

Expert advice for comfortable office setup.

Monitor Position

Place monitor at arm's length with top at eye level to prevent neck strain.

Chair Height

Adjust chair so feet rest flat on floor and knees at 90-degree angle.

Take Breaks

Stand and stretch every 30 minutes to maintain circulation and focus.

Keyboard Placement

Keep keyboard and mouse at elbow height to prevent wrist strain.
