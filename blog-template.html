What Clients Should Consider First

Give practical advice. Discuss budget, property type, room purpose, materials, durability, lifestyle, maintenance, lighting, installation and after-service support.

  • Start with the function of the room or property.
  • Choose finishes and products that match daily use.
  • Plan materials before installation begins.
  • Connect furniture choices to layout and storage.
  • Think about repairs and maintenance before problems grow.
Premium work is not only about how a space looks. It is also about how the space works, lasts, photographs, rents, sells and feels every day.
