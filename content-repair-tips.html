Plumbing Tips

Tip 1: A dripping tap wastes 20+ litres daily. Replace the washer early � it costs under KSh 200 and saves thousands in water bills.

Tip 2: Never use a plunger on a blocked sink with standing water and drain cleaner already added � chemical splash risk.

Tip 3: Toilet phantom flushing is usually a worn flapper � a 15-minute fix that can halve your water bill.

Electrical Tips

Tip 1: A breaker that keeps tripping means overloaded circuit � add sockets on a new circuit, don't just keep resetting.

Tip 2: Warm or discoloured sockets are a fire hazard. Stop using them immediately and call an electrician.

Tip 3: Flickering lights are usually a loose connection � not the bulb. Have wiring checked before it causes a fault.

Painting Tips

Tip 1: Never skip primer on fresh plaster or patched areas � paint without primer peels within months.

Tip 2: Dark patches reappearing through paint is damp from inside � treat the damp source, not just the surface.

Tip 3: Nairobi's rains mean exterior walls need weatherproof paint � standard interior paint will bubble and peel.

Furniture Repair Tips

Tip 1: Squeaky wardrobe hinges only need a drop of oil � don't replace the whole hinge unless it's bent.

Tip 2: Sagging sofa seats can be fixed with foam padding replacement � costs a fraction of a new sofa.

Tip 3: Swollen wood drawers in rainy season � rub a candle on the runners. Don't sand � they'll shrink back in dry season.
