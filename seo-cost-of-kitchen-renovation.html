KITCHEN RENOVATION COSTS

Price Breakdown

Understanding kitchen renovation costs in Nairobi.

Cabinet Refacing

Budget-friendly option: KES 15,000 - 50,000. Refresh cabinet doors without full replacement.

Countertop Replacement

Mid-range: KES 20,000 - 80,000. Granite, quartz, or laminate countertops.

Full Kitchen Remodel

Complete renovation: KES 100,000 - 500,000+. Full kitchen transformation.

BUDGET-FRIENDLY OPTIONS

Affordable Kitchen Upgrades

Cost-effective ways to transform your kitchen.

Paint Refresh

KES 5,000 - 15,000. A fresh coat of paint dramatically transforms cabinets and walls.

Hardware Update

KES 3,000 - 10,000. Replace handles, knobs, and pulls for an instant modern look.

Backsplash Installation

KES 8,000 - 25,000. Add tile or stone backsplash for visual impact.

Lighting Upgrade

KES 5,000 - 20,000. Under-cabinet and task lighting improve functionality.
