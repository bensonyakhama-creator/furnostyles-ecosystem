# Missing Pages Scan and Creation Report

**Date:** 2025
**Project:** Furnostyles Website
**Task:** Scan all internal links and create missing pages

## Executive Summary

A comprehensive scan of all HTML files, JavaScript files, header/footer data, marketplace data, and demo data was performed to identify missing pages linked throughout the Furnostyles website. The scan revealed that the site is well-developed with most pages already in place. Only one missing page was identified and created.
