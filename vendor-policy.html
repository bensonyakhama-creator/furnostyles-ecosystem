Vendor Policy

Last updated: May 2026  |  Effective: 1 January 2025

This Vendor Policy governs all sellers, suppliers, and service providers (“Vendors”) who list products or services on the Furnostyles Marketplace. By registering as a Vendor, you agree to comply with this policy in full.

1. Vendor Eligibility & Registration

To become a verified Furnostyles Vendor you must:

  • Be a legally registered business or individual trader operating in Kenya (or internationally for import/export listings).
  • Provide a valid business name, contact details, and proof of identity or business registration.
  • Complete the Furnostyles vendor verification process including a portfolio or product/service review.
  • Agree to these Vendor Policy terms and the Terms of Service.

2. Listing Standards

All listings on the Furnostyles Marketplace must meet the following standards:

  • Accuracy: Product/service descriptions, images, pricing, and availability must be accurate and not misleading.
  • Images: High-quality, original images of the actual product or service are required. Stock images may only be used for materials and consumables.
  • Pricing: Prices must be clearly stated in Kenyan Shillings (KES) and include all applicable fees.
  • Availability: Listings must reflect real-time stock or service availability. Inactive listings must be paused promptly.
  • Categories: Items must be listed in the correct category. Miscategorised listings will be removed.

The following are prohibited listings:

  • Counterfeit, stolen, or illegally obtained goods.
  • Hazardous materials without proper safety documentation.
  • Items prohibited under Kenyan law.
  • Duplicate listings for the same product.
  • Digital goods or downloads (unless pre-approved by Furnostyles).

3. Order Fulfilment Obligations

  • Vendors must fulfil all confirmed orders within the stated processing and delivery timelines.
  • Any delay exceeding 2 business days beyond the stated timeline must be communicated to the buyer and Furnostyles immediately.
  • Orders may not be cancelled by a Vendor after confirmation without prior written approval from Furnostyles. Repeated cancellations will result in suspension.
  • Vendors must pack products securely to prevent damage in transit.

4. Customer Service Standards

  • Respond to all customer enquiries within 24 hours on business days.
  • Handle complaints professionally and attempt resolution before escalating to Furnostyles.
  • Honour all confirmed returns eligible under the Return Policy.
  • Honour all warranty claims covered under the Warranty Policy.
  • Maintain a minimum average rating of 3.5/5 stars to remain listed.

6. Vendor Conduct

Vendors must not:

  • Contact buyers outside the Platform to circumvent Furnostyles fees or protections.
  • Solicit or post fake reviews.
  • Engage in price fixing or anti-competitive behaviour.
  • Share buyer personal data with third parties.
  • Make claims about Furnostyles endorsement beyond what is explicitly authorised.

7. Account Suspension & Termination

Furnostyles may suspend or permanently terminate a Vendor account for:

  • Repeated failure to fulfil orders on time.
  • Consistently poor customer ratings (below 3.0/5).
  • Verified fraud, misrepresentation, or policy violations.
  • Failure to comply with Buyer Protection resolutions.
  • Breach of these Vendor Policy terms.

Suspended vendors may appeal within 14 days by emailing furnostyles@gmail.com with supporting evidence.
