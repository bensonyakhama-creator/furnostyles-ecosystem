COMMON CEILING PROBLEMS

Why Ceilings Need Repair

Understanding ceiling damage causes.

Water Damage

Leaks from roofs or plumbing cause stains, sagging, and mold growth.

Cracks

Structural settling or temperature changes cause ceiling cracks.

Gypsum Damage

Impact damage or poor installation causes gypsum board failure.

CEILING REPAIR STEPS

How to Fix Ceiling Damage

Step-by-step ceiling repair guide.

Step 1: Identify the Cause

Locate the source of damage, especially water leaks, before repair.

Step 2: Remove Damaged Area

Cut out damaged section with clean edges for proper patching.

Step 3: Install Patch

Fit new gypsum board patch and secure with screws or adhesive.

Step 4: Finish and Paint

Apply joint compound, sand smooth, and paint to match existing ceiling.
