COMMON TAP PROBLEMS

Why Taps Leak

Understanding the causes of leaking taps.

Worn Washers

The most common cause of tap leaks is worn rubber washers that need replacement.

Loose Connections

Tap connections can loosen over time, causing water to drip from the base.

Valve Seat Damage

Corrosion or damage to the valve seat can prevent proper sealing.

DIY FIX STEPS

How to Fix a Leaking Tap

Step-by-step guide for basic tap repair.

Step 1: Turn Off Water

Locate the shut-off valve under the sink and turn it clockwise to stop water flow to the tap.

Step 2: Remove Tap Handle

Unscrew or pry off the tap handle carefully to access the internal mechanism.

Step 3: Replace Washer

Remove the old washer and replace it with a new one of the same size and type.

Step 4: Reassemble and Test

Put the tap back together, turn on the water, and check for leaks.
