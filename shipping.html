Shipping Information

Shipping Locations

We ship nationwide across Kenya including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, Thika, and other major towns.

Shipping Times

Nairobi: 2-3 business days
Other regions: 5-7 business days
Remote areas: 7-10 business days

Shipping Costs

Free shipping on orders above KSh 5,000
Standard shipping: KSh 500
Express shipping: KSh 1,000

Order Tracking

You can track your order status through your account or by contacting our support team with your order number.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within Kenya. International shipping will be available soon.
