To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Have return authorization from Furnostyles

The following items cannot be returned:
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Hygiene products (mattresses, pillows)

  • Items returned after the return period

  • Contact customer service within the return period
  • Provide order number and reason for return

5. Refund Methods

  • M-Pesa: Refund to the number used for payment
  • Bank Transfer: Refund to originating bank account
  • Card: Refund to original card (3-5 business days)

If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

Dispute Resolution Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Dispute Types

  • Product Quality: Item does not match description or is defective
  • Delivery Issues: Item not received, damaged in transit, or wrong item delivered

  • Service Quality: Service not performed to agreed standards
  • Vendor Conduct: Unprofessional behavior or communication issues

2. Dispute Filing Process

  • Contact customer service within 7 days of issue occurrence
  • Provide order number and detailed description of issue
  • Submit supporting evidence (photos, videos, correspondence)
  • Dispute ticket created and assigned to mediator

3. Mediation Process

  1. Initial Review: Furnostyles reviews evidence within 48 hours
  2. Vendor Response: Vendor given 48 hours to respond
  3. Evidence Review: Both parties submit additional evidence if needed
  4. Decision: Furnostyles makes final binding decision
  5. Resolution: Decision implemented within 5 business days

4. Resolution Outcomes

Possible resolutions include:
  • Full refund to customer
  • Partial refund
  • Product replacement
  • Store credit
  • Order cancellation
  • No action if claim is invalid

5. Escalation

If you disagree with the mediation decision, you may:

  • Request review by senior mediator within 7 days
  • Submit additional evidence for consideration
  • Pursue legal action through Kenyan courts (final option)

6. Time Limits

  • Disputes must be filed within 30 days of order delivery
  • Mediation process typically completes within 14 days
  • Escalation requests must be made within 7 days of initial decision
  • Legal action must be filed within 1 year of dispute

7. Costs

Mediation through Furnostyles is free of charge. However, legal action through courts is at the expense of the filing party. The prevailing party may be awarded legal fees.

8. Binding Nature

By using Furnostyles services, you agree that mediation decisions are binding and final. You waive the right to class action lawsuits and agree to resolve disputes individually.
