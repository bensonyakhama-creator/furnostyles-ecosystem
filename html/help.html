Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

Browse our marketplace, add items to cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay via M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Visa/Mastercard, and bank transfers. Pay on delivery is available for eligible orders.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location. Nairobi: 2-3 business days. Other regions: 5-7 business days.

Do you offer refunds or returns?

Yes, we offer returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible items. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
