Project Spotlight

Kilimani Luxury Apartment Transformation

A deep dive into one of our most comprehensive projects showcasing our full-service capabilities.

Project Overview

Complete transformation of a 4-bedroom apartment in Kilimani, Nairobi. The client requested a modern, luxurious living space that maximized natural light while incorporating smart home technology and premium finishes throughout.

The project involved complete interior redesign, custom furniture manufacturing, gypsum ceiling installation, kitchen renovation, and smart home integration.

Project Details

  • Location: Kilimani, Nairobi
  • Size: 3,500 sq ft
  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Budget: KES 8.5M
  • Team: 12 specialists

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

Limited natural light in the living area due to building orientation and existing window placement.

Solution

Installed reflective surfaces, light-colored finishes, and strategic LED lighting to enhance brightness and create an airy feel.

Challenge

Client desired seamless smart home integration without compromising aesthetic appeal.

Solution

Concealed wiring, touch panels with custom finishes, and voice-activated controls integrated into furniture design.
