Our Story

From Workshop to Premium Brand

Founded in 2022 with a simple vision: to bring premium interior design and craftsmanship to Nairobi homes.

What started as a small furniture workshop has grown into a comprehensive home solutions company serving clients across Kenya. Our journey has been guided by unwavering commitment to quality, attention to detail, and genuine passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces.

Today, Furnostyles stands as a trusted name in premium interiors, bespoke furniture, expert repairs, and complete home transformation services. Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers continues to push boundaries, delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations.

We specialize in luxury home interiors, custom furniture manufacturing, professional home repairs, gypsum ceiling installation, kitchen cabinetry, painting services, and construction finishing. Our expertise extends to residential interior design, commercial space planning, renovation projects, and property improvement solutions.
